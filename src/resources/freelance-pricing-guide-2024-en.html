The 3 Pricing Models

  1. Hourly/Daily Rate Pricing
    • Daily Rate: $500 - $1,000/day
    • Hourly Rate: $60 - $150/hour
    • Best for: short missions, support, maintenance
  2. Project-Based Pricing
    • Fixed price based on final deliverable
    • Business website: $2,500 - $10,000
    • E-commerce: $6,000 - $30,000
    • Web application: $12,000 - $60,000+
  3. Value-Based Pricing
    • Based on client ROI (return on investment)
    • Highest revenue potential
    • Requires excellent business understanding
